About

Hello! We are JDAN Enterprise LLC.

I (Jennifer Do) started my first project in maintenance and repair and facility cleaning for a printer company in South San Francisco in 2008 as a favor to a friend. The company needed facility maintenance, repair, and cleaning due to inconsistent performance from other companies.

In December 2010, my husband Hugh Do (pictured left) and I started a new company called JDAN Enterprise LLC. My background is in accounting and finance, but I also have extensive cleaning experience. Throughout my college years, I repaired and cleaned printers. I continued in this role for five years with my husband's assistance. Over the years, I received more referrals and started serving rental car companies throughout the Bay Area.

During the Covid-19 pandemic in June 2020, we were referred by our former customers to perform facility maintenance and equipment repair for rental car companies' equipment throughout the Bay Area.

In July 2021, we hired five full-time employees and two part-time employees to serve our customers. We clean buses, facilities, and repair equipment for the Rental Car Center and locations across the Bay Area.

I have been told that what makes me unique and gives me an advantage is that I understand businesses and know what my clients need and want. Despite my background in accounting and finance, I also manage operations. I am passionate about what I do, and the services I provide are not limited to a specific industry or company type. Therefore, opportunities for growth are unbounded, and I am always eager to discover what the future will bring.
